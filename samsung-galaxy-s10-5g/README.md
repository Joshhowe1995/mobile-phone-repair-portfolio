# Samsung Galaxy S10 5G Teardown and Repair

## Overview

This project documents a full teardown and reassembly of a Samsung Galaxy S10 5G.

The objective was to safely access internal components, manage battery disconnection, and verify device functionality after reassembly using a structured workflow.

---

## Initial Condition

- Device powered on successfully  
- Screen and touch functionality working  
- No visible external damage  

---

## Process

1. Verified device power and functionality  
2. Removed rear glass cover using controlled heat and prying tools  
3. Disconnected internal components and isolated the battery  
4. Removed internal shielding and accessed motherboard  
5. Extracted motherboard and inspected internal layout  
6. Reassembled device in reverse order  
7. Powered on device to confirm successful reassembly  

---

## Outcome

- Device successfully disassembled and reassembled  
- No damage to internal components  
- Device powered on and reached home screen after reassembly  

---

## Skills Demonstrated

- Hardware troubleshooting and verification  
- Safe device disassembly techniques  
- Battery handling and risk management  
- Component identification and organisation  
- Structured, step-by-step technical workflow
