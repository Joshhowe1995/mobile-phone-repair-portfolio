# Rear Cover Removal

## Objective

Gain internal access to the device by safely removing the rear cover.

## Actions Performed

- Applied controlled heat to loosen adhesive  
- Used plastic pry tools to separate rear cover  
- Carefully removed rear panel without damaging internal components  

## Findings

- Rear cover successfully removed  
- Internal components exposed with no damage  

## Conclusion

Device is ready for internal inspection and component access.
